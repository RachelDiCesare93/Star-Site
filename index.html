All about Angelica!

Ladies and Gentleman,Boys and Girls! My name is Angelica Pickles. I like to pick on the dumb babies,play with my favorite doll Cynthia! (Oh, and sometimes Susie Charmichael),eat lots of cookies,pet my cat Fluffy,and practice being SHINING STAR! Of course my daddy says that I have to share the spotlight with everybody else, but I am tired of practicing with the dumb babies,so I thought I will start my own SHOWBIZNESS! There are many things you can do to be a Shining Star, but my favorites are singing,dancing and acting! Even though I am the best, I will give lessons on how to do those 3 things!

I can hold lessons at the playground,friday,saturday and sunday. Make sure you tell your mommy and daddy to give you a microphone, dance shoes (ballet,tap and jazz) and a beret.I will provide dressup clothes,music,milk and cookies.You should bring some snacks too.We will do lessons for 3 hours, as I like to spend time playing with Cynthia.Before you enter my area you must pay a price of $5. We will have so much fun with my lessons because like I said, I'm the bestest in the whole wide world.

Singing

We will have singing lessons, with genres pop,hip-hop and folk! My favorite song is the "Cynthia Song".If you have a favorite song,you can use that too!

Dancing

The dances we will do in the lessons will be ,ballet,jazz and tap. No hokey-pokey allowed.But after we finish we can freestyle!

Acting

I have many scripts of plays and stories,we can even make movies.My favorite is Cinderella.Don't worry, there will be room for all the girls and boys to be princesses and princes.

So come one come all!
